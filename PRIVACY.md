# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 16, 2024**

## Introduction

Deduction Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide

When you use Deduction Tracker, you may enter:

- Expense information (amounts, descriptions, categories, dates)
- Mileage trip information (locations, distances, purposes)
- Receipt photos
- Business name (optional)

### Data Stored Locally

All your expense and mileage data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data.

### Purchase Information

When you subscribe or make a purchase, Apple processes the transaction. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but do not receive your payment details.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

- Display your expenses and mileage in the app
- Calculate your estimated tax deductions
- Generate reports for your personal use

## Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage systems. Your expense and mileage data never leaves your device unless you choose to export it.

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal data to third parties. Your financial information stays on your device.

## Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

- **Apple StoreKit**: For processing in-app purchases and subscriptions
- **Apple CloudKit** (if enabled): For syncing data across your devices

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access all data stored in the app
- Export your data at any time
- Delete all your data by deleting the app

## Data Retention

Your data is stored on your device for as long as you keep the app installed. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data.

## Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage systems.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

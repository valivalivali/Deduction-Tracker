# Deduction Tracker

A personal expense and mileage tracking app for freelancers and self-employed professionals.

## About

Deduction Tracker helps you organize your potential tax deductions throughout the year. Track your business expenses, log mileage trips, and see your estimated tax savings at a glance.

**Important:** This is a personal tracking tool for your own reference. It is NOT official tax software, does NOT file taxes, and does NOT replace a tax professional. Always consult a qualified accountant for tax advice.

## Features

- Track business expenses by category
- Log mileage trips with automatic IRS rate calculation
- See estimated tax savings on dashboard
- Attach receipt photos to expenses
- Quarterly tax deadline reminders
- Export reports for your accountant
- Light and dark mode support

## Expense Categories

The app uses IRS Schedule C categories including:

- Office expenses
- Travel
- Meals (50% deductible)
- Car and truck expenses
- Rent and lease
- Utilities
- Insurance
- Professional services
- Advertising
- And more
